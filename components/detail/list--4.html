By default, WP will not apply an inline style to ul or ol tags. Therefore, this setting is styled as a non-bulleted list. If ‘circle’, ‘disc’, or ‘square’ is selected for ‘Bulleted list’ in the editor, the inline style with that attribute will be added. ‘Numbered list’ in the WP editor does not have an alternative style.

<ul style="list-style-type: square;">
    <li>Consequatur rem ratione sit.</li>
    <li>Non sunt quis.</li>
    <li>Atque dolorem eum quibusdam quia et.</li>
    <li>Omnis enim consequatur architecto mollitia et quia.</li>
    <li>Placeat totam in placeat voluptatum sed.</li>
</ul>
 
{{!-- Read notes in list.config.js --}}
<ul style="list-style-type: square;">
  {{#each list}}
  <li>{{ list.text }}</li>
  {{/each}}
</ul>
