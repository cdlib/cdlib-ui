By default, WP will not apply an inline style to ul or ol tags. Therefore, this setting is styled as a non-bulleted list. If ‘circle’, ‘disc’, or ‘square’ is selected for ‘Bulleted list’ in the editor, the inline style with that attribute will be added. ‘Numbered list’ in the WP editor does not have an alternative style.

<ul style="list-style-type: disc;">
    <li>Est quia minus sit.</li>
    <li>Asperiores fugit aliquid enim aperiam saepe numquam repellat voluptatibus.</li>
    <li>Debitis adipisci et enim quia qui tempora neque corrupti.</li>
    <li>Accusamus ut deserunt ipsam consequuntur et aut deleniti.</li>
    <li>Quisquam molestias sunt est rerum eos ex corporis nemo qui.</li>
</ul>

<ul style="list-style-type: disc;">
  {{#each list}}
  <li>{{ list.text }}</li>
  {{/each}}
</ul>
